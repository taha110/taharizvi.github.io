Area of Darkness (VR)

Project Details


  • Release : 30-April-2019
  • Steam : Area of Darkness (VR)

    • Abstract :
     Area of Darkness: Sentinel is a psychological thriller/adventure game set in 1978. There are lots of interesting puzzles, immersive motion controller based combat system and lots of exploration and adventure.

    My Responsibilities:

    As a lead game developer, I had several responsibilities as mentioned below:


  • Managing source control using Perforce.
  • Interaction and collaboration with TechArt , Animation and 3D modelling teams.
  • Development of Full body character (no floating hands here), Hand and foot IK.
  • Making wrist and arm twists joint simulate like real.
  • Quick Time Events (QTE) motion controller based action combat system.
  • A physical logbook to keep game notes for total VR immersion.
  • Developed unique puzzles using a variety of techniques such as controller motion, item combining, physics-based throwing, and environmental manipulation.

    • Techinical Details:

  • Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Developed for PC VR (Oculus, vive and windows mixed reality). Will be ported on PS-VR and non-VR later.
  • Used Blueprints and C++ both, as per our requirement.

    • Other Project Details:

  • Breathtaking locations to explore: from an upstate New York mansion to millennia-old tombs on the Sentinel Islands
  • Experience thrilling, life-like set pieces built from the group up for VR, including the sensation of parachuting from a plane at 30,000 feet!
  • Interact with a vibrant cast of characters that all play an essential role in the unfolding mystery
  • Fight supernatural creatures using the intuitive, motion-based combat system
  • Solve unique puzzles using a variety of techniques such as controller motion, item combining, physics-based throwing, and environmental manipulation
  • Collect dozens of fully voiced journal entries, telling an exciting sub-narrative of those who came before...
  • Fully motion-captured scenes choreographed using professional actors and voice actors, with animation created by an Emmy-nominated Animation Director
  • Smooth VR-optimized movement and rotation options mean no motion sickness!
  • Cinematic movement and rotation options for the VR Purists out there - feel the intensity!
